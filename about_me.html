Hi, I'm Sidd!


My name is Segun Olatunbosun. I fondly like to be called Sidd. I am a Software Developer based in Lagos, Nigeria who loves mobile experiences and Artificial Intelligence.

I currently practice my proffession as a Software Developer where I build Webpages, Exclusive Websites and Web Applications. I am already currently enrolled in Moat Academy, Ikeja Lagos.

My non-tech passion includes education & music. I believe everyone should be given a chance at basic education. Because of this, I joined Lend A Hand Of Hope (NGO). There, we are helping children and yound adults underserved communities gain access to education and skill acquisition so that they can compete for global opportunities.

In tech, I am a huge fan of great mobile experiences driven by AI. At a tech event, you'd probably find me in a booth with a VR headset on, then if not at a Tech event, I am certainly in the Studio Making Music.
